cult with the aspirations of attaining its goal of "The New Society" and
thusforth overachieving its expectations.

Goal. First and foremost, billions of acres of rain forest are currently

substances, such as smog, to latch onto its molecules and thusforth, smother
the inhabitants of the Earth. Our mission is to prove that we can do without
this horrid element, as we have developed a so-called "cocktail" of Unununium
and Nitrogen which will take its place. As we continue to burn acres of the
rain forests on Bolivian soil, we are reaching our goal faster than ever.
